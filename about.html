We are an education platform welcoming students from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the current industry scenarios and trends. We believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world.

We connect academics with industry by addressing the problem of lack of adequate professionals to cope with the industry standards. We plan and execute events to facilitate learning & research, promote knowledge transfer, provide exposure to and awareness of the field, address current industry needs and trends and strengthen the bridge between industry and institutions.

In fact, everything we do is driven by this goal: Evolve educational programs with the broad requirements of the industry and give rise to professionals capable of coping up with the accelerating pace of technological development.
